Services

We offer a range of services tailored to elevate your brand and connect with your audience. Our packages are designed to meet your needs, whether you're starting fresh or looking to enhance your current branding.

CREATION PACKAGE

Our Creation package is perfect for businesses looking to make a bold entrance or revamp their existing presence.

Includes:
1. Initial consultation and strategy session.
2. Concept development and creative brainstorming.
3. Custom content creation (up to 3 campaign ideas).
4. One round of revisions based on feedback.

BRANDING  PACKAGE

Ideal for businesses seeking a comprehensive brand identity.

Includes:
1. Brand consultation and market research.
2. Logo design and branding guidelines 
3. Brand voice and messaging strategy.
4. Business card and letterhead design.

DESIGN  PACKAGE

Our Design package is suited for those who need eye-catching visuals to communicate their message.

Includes:
1. Graphic design for up to 5 assets
2. Two design concepts to choose from.
3. Up to two rounds of revisions on selected design.
4. Final assets provided in multiple formats.

PROMOTION  PACKAGE

Perfect for launching a new product or boosting your brand's visibility.

Includes:
1. Development of a promotional strategy.
2. Creation of promotional materials (digital and print).
3. Social media campaign setup (up to 3 platforms).
4. Analytics and performance report post-campaign.
