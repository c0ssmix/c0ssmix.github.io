About us

How did we start all of this?

From the very beginning, our journey has been intertwined. Yes, you read that right – we're not just creative partners; we're family. Cousins, to be exact. Our bond goes beyond mere creativity, it's rooted in a shared history and a lifelong friendship.

Our collaborative magic started early. From joint drawing sessions to playful dreams of running a business – remember our 'perfume shop'? – we've always sparked off each other. And eventually, this synergy blossomed into something bigger: Digital Cherries – an Advertising and PR agency.

We're not here to tug at your heartstrings. Instead, we want you to understand the depth of our unique partnership and how it can propel your brand to new heights. Our journey with graphic design began in 2013, and we haven't stopped since, constantly expanding our knowledge with bachelor's and master's degrees in the field.

Our passion for making a significant impact started over a decade ago. We're young, energetic, and ready to bring a fresh, dynamic twist to your business. So, how about it? Are you ready to partner with a team that can turn your vision into reality?
